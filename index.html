Do you only take reservations?
We recommend making prior reservations since walk-ins are welcome only subject to th availability of tables. In case you are hosting a gathering of people more than 5, please reach out to the team to make your reservations at +91 658 467 2348

Are children allowed at Anjeer?
Children under 12 years are not permitted in the restaurant however, our sister restaurant Fig Red which is in the same complex welcomes them +91 658 467 2348
Do you offer dining for larger groups?
We can make a provision that can seat up to 10 people. You can call us at +91 658 467 2348, and you will be guided per your requirements. Please note that we do not host private and corporate events.
How can I make reservations?
You can make a reservation by calling us at +91 658 467 2348 or through the website. Note: We only hold the table for 15 minutes from the time of your reservation.
Is there parking available?
A common parking space is available in the basement or right when you enter the complex. There is a valet facility as well.
